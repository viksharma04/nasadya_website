Our Story

"Having witnessed firsthand how a huge disparity in access to energy can limit access to opportunities and a chance at an improved life, it's my life goal to develop energy systems that ensure clean, sustainable energy for all. Moreover, I feel clean and sustainable energy access is the most important problem I need to solve in order to pursue my vision of creating a society that is driven by passion. After identifying excess energy from variability in the grid as a huge problem, I launched a startup called NASADYA." -Chaitanya Gulati, CEO
